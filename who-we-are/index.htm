The origin

Founded in 2017 in Dubai, UAE, Al Sabahia General Trading LLC specializes in wholesale and retail of cosmetics, household essentials, cleaning products, detergents, and food items, with a strong distribution network and skilled sales team ensuring quality delivery.

Al Sabahia General Trading LLC maintains strong partnerships with leading brands in cosmetics, detergents, cleaning products, and food items, securing reliable contracts with major suppliers. This commitment to a consistent supply chain, as highlighted by our leadership, supports our vision to foster enduring partnerships and drive innovation, meeting the dynamic needs of the UAE market.

Beyond local offerings, Al Sabahia imports high-demand goods tailored to UAE trends and is pursuing exclusive agency agreements with global brands. As of 2025, this expansion aligns with Dubai’s growing e-commerce sector and sustainability focus, reinforcing our goal to deliver excellence across the region.
